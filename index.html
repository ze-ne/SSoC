Society for Scientists of Color

About us

Society for Scientists of Color is an RSO dedicated to providing a space for people of color within STEM to connect to, learn from, and grow with each other.

Community

SSoC aims to provide a sense of community and belonging between Scientists of Color.

Mentorship

SSoC facilitiates mentorship between undergrads so that students can have acess to people who have navigated similar academic and professional experiences.

Resources

SSoC promotes and ensures the continued growth of accessibility and opportunity for students of color interested in science, technology, engineering and mathematics.

Resources

A collection of resources to help you navigate STEM as a scientist of color. Look here for scholarships, information sessions, and other ways to connect and thrive in this community.

Family Resources

Within this folder, resources are separated by major. Each folder includes resources such as contact information, internship/scholarship opportunities, and useful links. If you have questions about specific resources or suggestions to add, reach out to your family head. CLICK HERE FOR RESOURCES!

Family Heads

For major specific advice, feel free to reach out to your family head(s). Family heads are uperclassmen within your major dedicated to helping provide advice, resources and support. They made mistakes so you don't have to.

For general information or questions regarding family heads and their resources, reach out to Nikita Mehta (nikitamehta@uchicago.edu), the family head program chair.
